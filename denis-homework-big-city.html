🏙️ Life in a Big City

Many people today live in big cities. Cities like London, New York, and Tokyo are home to millions of people. Life in a big city is exciting, but it is also difficult.

In a big city, there are many things to do. There are restaurants, museums, and parks. There is good public transport, so you don't need a car. Many companies are in big cities, so it is easy to find a job.

But big cities also have problems. Traffic jams are very common. In the morning and evening, the roads are very busy — this is called rush hour. Pollution is also a big problem: cars and factories make the air dirty.

Many software developers and tech workers choose to live in big cities. There are many tech companies and co-working spaces. The internet is fast and the offices are modern. It is a great place to build a tech career.

Q1. Name TWO things you can do in a big city.
✅ For example: you can go to restaurants, museums, or parks. You can find a job easily. You can use public transport.
Q2. What is "rush hour"? Explain in your own words.
✅ Rush hour is when the roads are very busy — in the morning and evening when people travel to and from work.
Q3. Why do software developers like big cities? Give ONE reason.
✅ Because there are many tech companies, co-working spaces, and modern offices. The internet is fast. It is easy to build a career.
Q4. Name TWO problems in big cities.
✅ Traffic jams and pollution are two big problems in cities.
Q5. TRUE or FALSE?  "You always need a car in a big city."
