  • The party went on much longer than expected
  • Your phone battery died — you couldn't call
  • You didn't want to leave your friends early
Explain what happened and try to agree on rules for the future.

📝 Examiner's Tips
What FCE examiners look for in Speaking Part 3
✓React to your partner — don't just wait for your turn
✓Make suggestions: "Perhaps we should…", "What if we…?"
✓Use discourse markers: "Well…", "The thing is…", "Actually…"
✓Try to reach a decision or compromise by the end
✓Show empathy: "I understand how you feel about that"
✓Avoid repeating the same words — aim for a range of vocabulary
